Amanita Muscaria

The Cute Red Mushroom

White spotted

The Amanita Muscaria, also known as fly agaric, is the name of the cute red mushroom that we often see depicted in fairy tales.

Poison

The Amanita Muscaria is poisonous. Although death is rare, it is not recommended for eating.

Why is it called Fly Agaric?

These mushrooms are traditionally used for catching flies.

Dreams

Do you remember Alice in Wonderland? Everytime she ate a mushroom she would transform. The Amanita Muscaria has been known for having psychoactive effects on people.
